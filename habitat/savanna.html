Savanna Habitats

Savannas are characterized by open grasslands with scattered trees, often found in tropical regions.

Examples of Savanna Animals

  • Lion – Known as the "king of the jungle," lions are social big cats that live in prides.
  • Giraffe – The tallest land animal, known for its long neck and ability to reach high branches.
  • Elephant – The largest land mammal, known for its intelligence and strong social bonds.
