Wetlands Habitats

Wetlands are areas where water covers the soil, providing unique habitats for a variety of plants and animals.

Examples of Wetland Animals

  • American Alligator – A large reptile known for its powerful jaws and stealthy hunting abilities.
  • Great Blue Heron – A large wading bird known for its long legs and striking appearance.
  • Beaver – Known for building dams and lodges, beavers are important ecosystem engineers.
