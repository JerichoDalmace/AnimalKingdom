Desert Habitats

Deserts are dry, arid regions with sparse vegetation, extreme temperatures, and unique wildlife adapted to harsh conditions.

Examples of Desert Animals

  • Fennec Fox – A small fox with large ears, known for its ability to thrive in the Sahara Desert.
  • Camel – Known as the "ship of the desert," these animals can endure long periods without water.
  • Horned Lizard – A small reptile that can blend into its surroundings to avoid predators.
